How to replace placeholders with your real models

  1. Replace the placeholder parameters in app.js with values estimated from your own NIFTY data.
  2. Swap the simple model formula with your trained multimodal model output.
  3. Replace the transition matrices with those built from your Markov regime classification.
  4. Update the VIX threshold logic with your empirically estimated VIX states and conditional matrices.
